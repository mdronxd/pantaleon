Niagara Falls


Niagara Falls is the collective name for three waterfalls that straddle the international border between Canada and the United States.

Yosemite Falls


Yosemite Falls is the highest waterfall in Yosemite National Park, dropping a total of 2,425 feet from the top of the upper fall to the base of the lower fall.

Iguazu Falls


The Iguazu Falls, Iguazú Falls, Iguassu Falls, or Iguaçu Falls are waterfalls of the Iguazu River.

Angel Falls


Angel Falls is a waterfall in Venezuela. It is the world's highest uninterrupted waterfall, with a height of 979 meters and a plunge of 807 meters.
